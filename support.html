Common Questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Open Settings on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → RideForgeApp. You can change plans or cancel there at any time.

How do I restore a previous purchase?
Open RideForgeApp, go to the subscription screen, and tap "Restore Purchases."

How many free rides do I get?
The free plan includes 3 AI-forged rides per month. Pro removes the limit.

How do I report a problem in the app?
Use the Feedback button in the More tab, or email us directly.
